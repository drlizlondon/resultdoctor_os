ALP — requires GGT (Paired variable) · Source: Page 2 · EXPLICIT
Explicitly stated in PDF
"Isolated cholestatic liver enzymes and not bone related: ALP and GGT. Isolated ALP: Consider drug or bone causes including Vit D deficiency."
IFALP is raised
CONTEXT REQUIREDGGT must be checked before ALP can be interpreted in this pathway
IFGGT also raised → liver aetiology → proceed to liver aetiology screen
IFGGT NOT raised → bone/drug aetiology → consider bone causes including Vitamin D deficiency
